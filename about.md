# About

My work is strongest where business ambiguity, reporting needs, and governance requirements meet. I help organizations turn messy business needs into decision-ready analytics and trusted reporting.

I am most useful in environments where reporting is fragmented, metrics are debated, or teams need a clearer path from business question to repeatable analytics delivery.

## What I do best

- Reporting modernization and executive scorecards
- Governance, controls, and metric trust
- Building repeatable analytics workflows and documentation
- Cross-functional delivery and stakeholder alignment

## Core strengths

- Bringing structure to messy reporting and process environments
- Translating between business stakeholders and technical delivery teams
- Designing KPI definitions, validation approaches, and operating rhythms that hold up over time
- Leading through hands-on execution, documentation, and practical standards

## How I work

I balance business context, pragmatic engineering, and clear standards. I aim for solutions that teams can maintain and leaders can trust.

## Tools & platforms

SQL, Azure, Power BI, Python, SAS, Git, and enterprise reporting and metadata tooling.

## Professional approach

I prefer direct language, measurable outcomes, and solutions that improve both reporting quality and team operating discipline.
